Didot & Futura PT

Didot and Futura PT are my choices for fonts with Didot used largely for headers and titles and Futura used as main body font including links and urls. Didot and Futura are both classy fonts with Didot obviously being serif and Futura being sans-serif. They pair together to create a typographic look that is visually simple & clean yet relatively easy to read as well.
