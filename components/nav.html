Navbar


Overview & Usage

The Navigation bar will be simple. The logo will be on the left whereas the the actual actionable items will be on the right. The navigation bar will remain fixed at the top of the page even as the user scrolls.


Accessibility

Ensure that items are easily pressable on mobile. The hamburger menu's pressable area should be at least 36x36px. Add hover states to the links to ensure users know that they are interacting with them.
